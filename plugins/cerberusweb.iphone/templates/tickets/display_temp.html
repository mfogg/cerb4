All About PogoNotes
PogoNotes are a free and simple web-based way to stay organized using your iPhone. They are accessible anywhere you go (just stay in cell range, please), and you can co-edit your notes with your friends.
To co-edit, just click "share" and type in the username of your friend. The note will then show up on their personal list. To share with a group of people, type the group name in (you can make new groups up on the fly). Your friends can then veiw all the notes in that group.
